Art

Making things and drawing have been a part of my life since I was a child. The techniques have (hopefully) gotten better, but my childlike curiosity of filtering what I see remains a deep part of my experience as a human. Below the art is sorted by date. You can also choose a category on the right to see art related only to that category.

If you are interested in art, science and adventure check out my Montology Zine series. You can order zines through my Etsy Shop or Sign-up for the newsletter if you want to stay in the loop on new issues.

I co-founded a gallery/classroom/studio space in Dunsmuir, CA called Darmera Studios. Darmera is an art studio dedicated to the natural world and community. We opened our doors May 4, 2024. Sign-up for our newsletter containing information about gallery shows, classes, workshops, and events.
