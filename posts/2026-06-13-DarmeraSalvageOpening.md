The current show at [www.darmera.studio](https://www.darmera.studio/) is titled **Salvage**. The show features original photographs and paintings by Sean Julian, Amanda Thomas and Chris Clother. This trio teaches art classes at College of the Siskiyous. The show runs from June 13 - August 1, 2026.
