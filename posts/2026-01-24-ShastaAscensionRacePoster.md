This was a really fun commission. The challenge was to come up with a paired theme that could work for both the Ascension back-country ski race as well as the Snowball later that evening. The other challenge was pre-planning how all the images would be cut up for sharing on various platforms that require different sizes and aspect ratios. Most of the art is from my imagination. It felt like I really stepped up my design game on this one.
