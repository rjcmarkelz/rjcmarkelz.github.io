I am giving a few talks about my [**Siskiyou Crest Coalition Artist in Residence**](https://siskiyoucrestcoalition.org/siskiyou-crest-artist-in-residence-program/) experience at the Acorn Woman Lookout in Southern Oregon. I will be releasing my illustrated zine of the experience.

[**A very fun introductory video**](https://vimeo.com/1202675432?fl=pl&fe=sh) by Suzie Savoie 

"Cody spent 5 days at Acorn Woman Lookout in the Rogue River Siskiyou National Forest this May, making observations, journal entries and drawings.  He’s compiled them into a zine!"
