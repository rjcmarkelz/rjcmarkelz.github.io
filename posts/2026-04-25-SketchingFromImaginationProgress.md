### Sketch from memory, design from imagination
I took a 9 week Sketching from Imagination class with [Sorie Kim](https://www.instagram.com/soriekim/). After studying some subjects in detail here are some update sketches from memory/imagination. I am trying out similar poses to see how things vary between species, then I will be able to switch up the pose and do something more interesting. Overall, I am happy with my progress over doodling just shapes.
