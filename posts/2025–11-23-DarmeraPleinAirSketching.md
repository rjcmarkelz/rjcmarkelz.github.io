While things were winding down at [Darmera](https://www.darmera.studio/) for the season, Davis and I spent the late fall and early winter weekends outside sketching and painting. It was fun to reconnect over our love for art. With all happenings at Darmera during the past few years it was hard for us to find time to hang out and make art. Davis set his painting down on the train to keep it safe while he packed up. He forgot it! A free Davis Elliot art piece out there in the wild for anyone who is interested.
